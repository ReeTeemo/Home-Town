A few of the many activities to do in Swindon

Steam - Museum of the Great Western Railway

A railway network that, through the pioneering vision and genius of Isambard Kingdom Brunel, was regarded as the most advanced in the world.

Lydiard Park

A beautiful historic estate on the western edge of Swindon, with the Palladian House, Church and Walled Garden, set in 260 acres of parkland.

Wyvern Theatre

A 635 seat venue hosting musicals, comedy, plays and gigs.
